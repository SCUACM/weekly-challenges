Week 4: Zig Zag

Given a string and a height for a zig zag pattern, zig zag the string and print it in zig-zag form.

Example: zig_zag("COFFEE", 3)

Zig-zagged form:  C  E
	          O F E
	          F

Output: CEOFEF
